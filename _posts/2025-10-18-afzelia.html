Propagation

Afzelia can be propagated through various methods:

  • Seeds: While possible, seed germination can be slow and unpredictable
  • Softwood cuttings: More reliable method, especially in temperate regions
  • Hardwood cuttings: Less commonly used, but possible
  • Grafting: Used in some commercial operations
  • Layering: Can be effective in certain conditions

For successful propagation, particularly of softwood cuttings, it's recommended to take cuttings in late winter or early spring when the tree is dormant.
