About Us

Dedicated to Improving Human Health and Wellness

The Heritage Roadmap Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving human health and wellness through innovative, evidence-based interventions. We operate through two collaborative programs that address critical global health challenges: nutrition-sensitive sustainable agriculture and digital health solutions for chronic non-communicable diseases.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To improve human health and wellness through collaborative programs that combine nutrition and sustainability with cutting-edge digital health solutions, making a meaningful and lasting positive impact on the lives of marginalized and underserved communities.

Our Vision

A world where all communities have access to sustainable nutrition, quality healthcare, and digital health innovations that empower them to lead healthier, more prosperous lives regardless of their socioeconomic status.

Our Core Values

Impartiality

We serve all communities without bias or discrimination.

Humanity

We prioritize compassion and human dignity in all our work.

Neutrality

We maintain independence in our interventions and advocacy.

Accountability

We ensure transparent and ethical use of resources.

Our Operational Programs

Heritage Eco-AgriVenture

Our first operational wing focuses on promoting sustainable and nutritious agriculture among marginalized communities. We implement practices that enhance food security while protecting environmental resources.

Focus Areas: Nutrition-sensitive agriculture, community training, sustainable farming practices, food security initiatives.

Wilmah Eco-Health Care

Our second operational wing specializes in research, development, and deployment of digital health solutions. We focus on providing healthcare products and services for individuals with chronic non-communicable diseases.

Focus Areas: Digital health innovation, eco-health research, chronic disease management, telehealth services.

Fundraising & Community Events

Heritage Charity Golf Classic and Wilmah Charity Golf Classic serve as primary fundraising initiatives that support our programs while building community engagement and awareness of our mission.

Focus Areas: Community engagement, donor development, awareness raising, sustainable funding.

Our Approach

Heritage Roadmap Foundation operates through an agile methodology that emphasizes specialization, accountability, and efficient management. By creating distinct operational wings, we ensure that each unit can focus on its area of expertise while maintaining transparent, results-oriented practices. We believe in coordinated and integrated approaches to healthcare interventions that help communities build resilience and recover from health crises effectively.
