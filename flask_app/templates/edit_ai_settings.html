About These Settings

These settings control how the AI generates plant articles from research data:

  • Embedding Model: Converts text to vectors for semantic understanding and retrieval
  • LLM Model: Generates human-readable article content from research data
  • Front Matter: Jekyll-compatible metadata (title, date, layout)
  • Images: Fetches botanical images from Wikimedia Commons
  • Device: Determines where computations happen (CPU is slow but universal; GPU is fast)
